About our Covid-19 protocals

Protecting nursing home residents from COVID-19 has been a top priority since the onset of the pandemic. To ensure their safety, nursing homes have implemented stringent measures that include regular testing for residents and staff, strict visitation protocols, and isolation procedures for those who test positive. Additionally, vaccination campaigns have played a pivotal role in safeguarding this vulnerable population, with residents and staff receiving priority access to vaccines.
