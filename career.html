Careers

At Comfort Foods we believe in the power of delicious food to bring seniors together. Our commitment to culinary excellence, exceptional service, and a passion for creating memorable dining experiences are at the heart of everything we do. We are thrilled that you are considering a career with us!

Why Choose a Career with Comfort Foods?

  • A Culinary Journey: Join a team of dedicated nurses, chefs and food enthusiasts who are passionate about creating exceptional dishes for seniors. Whether you're a seasoned chef or just starting your culinary career, you'll have the opportunity to learn and grow with us.
  • Hospitality Excellence: We pride ourselves on providing top-notch service to our seniors. Join our front-of-house team and be part of an environment where feeding seniors and proper nutrition is our top priority.
  • Diverse Opportunities: From kitchen roles to management positions, we offer a wide range of career opportunities. Whether you're interested in cooking, management, or nursing there's a place for you here.
  • A Collaborative Environment: We foster a culture of teamwork and collaboration. When you join us, you become part of a family that supports each other's growth and success.
  • Innovation and Creativity: We encourage creativity in the kitchen and beyond. We want to hear from you if you have innovative ideas or a unique approach to the culinary world.

What we offer:

  • Competitive compensation packages
  • Comprehensive training and development programs
  • Opportunities for career advancement
  • A supportive and inclusive work environment
  • Employee discounts and perks
  • A commitment to sustainability and community involvement

Rewarding Career

We want to hear from you if you're ready to embark on a rewarding career in the food service industry and share our passion for great food and hospitality for the older community. Explore our current job openings and submit your application online. We look forward to getting to know you and discovering how you can contribute to our culinary journey.

At Comfort Foods we're not just creating dishes; we're crafting experiences. Join us and be a part of something extraordinary.

We are looking for the following:

  • Chefs
  • Line cooks
  • Serves and food nutrition Aides
  • Managers and Supervisor
  • Nurses
  • Accountant and Financial Aides
